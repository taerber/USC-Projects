Problems in Stock
Stock price prediction has great significance in the field of business and finance. The stock price is one of the most important measurements for investors to evaluate the performance of certain companies and to make wise decisions for their future investments. Financial groups themselves also value stock price as it represents the profitability and leadership in the market. Even though the stock price is fluctuating, the machine learning algorithms are able to have a relatively reasonable prediction on it. In this project, we introduce several machine learning models for predicting stock prices and design a user interface for people who are not familiar with machine learning to use these models to get the result of future stock price
Analysis of a Stock
Our analysis includes predictions from these models: Linear Regression, K Nearest Neighbors (KNN), XGBoost (extreme gradient boosting), and LSTM (Long short-term memory). As well as, different features(training error, testing error, accuracy, and MSE) to compare across the models and data will also be displayed.
